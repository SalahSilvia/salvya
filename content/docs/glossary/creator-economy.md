## DH commission

Fixed Moroccan Dirham amount earned **per item sold**, tiered by Instagram follower band.

## Attribution

Process linking a checkout order to the creator promo link that referred the fan.

## Promo link

Trackable URL generated in the creator workspace pointing to a product or artist page.
